FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I access the content after subscribing?

▼

After subscribing via PayPal, you'll receive an email with instructions on how to access your exclusive content. Typically, you'll get a unique access link or login credentials within 24 hours.

Can I cancel anytime?

▼

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your PayPal account. No contracts, no commitments. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How often is new content added?

▼

New content is added regularly as Kenneth works on new projects. Behind The Build subscribers get updates throughout the build process, while Full Tutorial Access subscribers get complete tutorials when projects are finished.

Can I upgrade from Behind The Build to Full Tutorial Access?

▼

Yes! You can cancel your current subscription and sign up for the higher tier at any time. Contact us if you need help with the transition.

Is the content legal and ethical?

▼

Absolutely. All content focuses on authorized testing, educational use, and ethical hacking practices. Kenneth emphasizes legal use and responsible disclosure throughout all tutorials.

What if I have questions about building something?

▼

Full Tutorial Access subscribers get Q&A support via email. Kenneth will help troubleshoot issues and answer questions about the build process.
