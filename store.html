✅ Fully Assembled ✅ No Soldering Required ✅ Ready to Go Out of the Box ✅ Tripod Included

Product Description

This is a 2.4 GHz cantenna designed for Wi-Fi range extension. It's especially useful for those seeking much-needed longer range, and it's ideal for pentesters (penetration testing, ethical hackers, white hat hackers). Or anyone else looking for range out of their Wi-Fi adapter.

It works very well, providing great range and excellent penetration through walls for a reliable connection. I build these myself and ensure a professional finish.

Technical Specifications

  • Connection Type: Female N-type connector. You will need a Male N-type to SMA connector for your Wi-Fi card.
  • Build Quality: Accurately built, adhering to the 1/4 wavelength guide for the can's diameter. This ensures precise functionality (it's not just a can with a random metal pole placed inside).
  • Gain: 14dBi gain
  • Beam Width: 22-degree beam
  • Range: Up to 300 meters
  • Performance: Performs well through obstacles like trees, effective without direct line of sight
  • Mount: 1/4" thread mount (NOT 3/8")
