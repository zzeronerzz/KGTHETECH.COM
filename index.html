WHO IS KG THE TECH?

Kenneth Gilliam is a self-taught cybersecurity specialist from New York, now based in Aurora, Colorado. He builds fully functional ethical hacking tools from scratch — CANTennas, wardrivers, ESP32 development kits, and cyberdecks.

Kenneth grew up in poverty, faced homelessness, suffered three back injuries, and still creates professional-grade tools with his bare hands. He sources parts on a budget, builds devices that work better than commercial options, and documents everything on TikTok.

This is hacking meets hustle. Street money meets street code. From nothing to something.
