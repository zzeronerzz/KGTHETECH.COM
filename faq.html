📦 PRODUCTS & SPECIFICATIONS

What's included with each product?

▼

ESP32 Kit: ESP32 board, GPS antenna, 2x Wi-Fi antennas, MicroSD card (pre-formatted), wiring harness, quickstart guide.

Standard CANTenna: Assembled cantenna (black finish), tripod, N-to-SMA cable.

Heavy-Duty CANTenna: Heavy-duty cantenna (exhaust pipe construction), tripod, N-to-SMA cable.

All products are fully assembled and ready to use out of the box.

Are these products legal to own and use?

▼

Yes, for authorized testing, educational purposes, and use on networks/systems you own or have explicit written permission to test.

Unauthorized interception, access, or monitoring of communications or networks is ILLEGAL under federal law in the United States and many other countries.

Buyer assumes 100% of responsibility for lawful use. See the full legal disclaimer on the store page.

Do I need any additional equipment or licenses?

▼

For CANTennas: You'll need a Wi-Fi adapter with an external antenna port. A Male N-type to SMA adapter cable is included.

For ESP32 Kit: You'll need a Micro-USB cable for power and a computer for programming (if customizing firmware).

Radio Frequency Licenses: Use of RF devices may require appropriate licenses depending on your jurisdiction. It's your responsibility to comply with all local regulations.

How accurate are the cantenna specs?

▼

Kenneth builds every cantenna following strict 1/4 wavelength specifications. This isn't just a can with a random metal pole — it's precisely tuned for 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi frequencies.

14dBi gain, 22-degree beam width, and up to 300 meters range are real specifications, not marketing hype.

What's the difference between the standard and heavy-duty cantenna?

▼

Standard: Built from a soup can, professionally finished, accurate specs. Great for most users.

Heavy-Duty: Built from thick car exhaust pipe tubing. Extremely durable, weatherproof, virtually indestructible. Same RF performance, but tank-built construction.

Both perform identically in terms of signal. The heavy-duty version is for those who want maximum durability.

🛠️ TECHNICAL SUPPORT

My ESP32 kit isn't working. What should I do?

▼

First, check the quickstart guide included with your kit. Common issues:

  • Ensure the device is powered (5V via Micro-USB)
  • Verify antenna connections are secure
  • Check that the MicroSD card is properly inserted

If the issue persists, email KGtechsupport@kgthetech.com with a description of the problem and photos if applicable.

Can you help me set up or configure my device?

▼

Basic setup support is included with purchase. Email KGtechsupport@kgthetech.com with specific questions.

For advanced configuration, consider subscribing to the Full Tutorial Access tier, which includes Q&A support and detailed tutorials.

Can I customize the firmware on the ESP32 kit?

▼

Yes! The ESP32 kit is open-hardware. The minimal demo firmware is a starting point. You can program it with Arduino IDE, PlatformIO, or any ESP32-compatible toolchain.

Important: Any custom firmware you develop must comply with all applicable laws. Kenneth provides no support for unlawful applications.
